About me

I am an enthusiastic individual with a deep passion for video games and a keen interest in the gaming industry. With a track record that includes achieving of Master in 'League of Legends' and extensive experience in various other games, I've developed a comprehensive understanding of game mechanics, player engagement, and the importance of innovation in this dynamic field.

I also have a strong foundation in Japanese, allowing me to connect with a broader global gaming community and understand diverse cultural nuances in game design. In addition, I hold a Game Design Associate Degree, which has equipped me with a solid knowledge base in the fundamental principles of game design.

As a recent graduate with a strong desire to make my mark in the gaming industry, I am eagerly seeking my first opportunity to contribute my skills and passion to a dynamic game design team. My commitment to continuous learning and adaptability has been honed through my experiences in the competitive gaming community. I believe in the power of diversity and inclusion and look forward to applying my skills and knowledge to create exceptional gaming experiences that resonate with players. My goal is to ensure that beloved games like 'League of Legends' remain relevant and captivating for years to come.
